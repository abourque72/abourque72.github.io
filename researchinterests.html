Andrea Bourque

I used to study mathematical physics with Anton Zeitlin. See our paper "Flat GL(1|1)-connections and fatgraphs" here. This paper was published to the Journal of Geometry of Physics, Volume 191.

I am currently working with Pramod Achar to give an explicit description of global Schubert varieties for GL(n), using lattices. So far, we have a description for global Schubert varieties indexed miniscule coweights. The work can be extended in several ways: getting similar results for all dominant coweights, not just miniscule ones; using our descriptions to understand resolutions of singularities of global Schubert varieties; extending our results to other groups. There is also a family of open subsets of the special fiber of global Schubert varieties (at least in the miniscule case). I would like to show that they have nice properties, both intrinsic and in relation to the stratification by orbits.

My broad interests are in algebraic geometry, representation theory, and number theory. I also would like to explore interest in homotopy theory and algebraic K theory.
