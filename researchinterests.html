I used to study mathematical physics with Anton Zeitlin. See our preprint "Flat GL(1|1)-connections and fatgraphs" here. This paper will appear in the Journal of Geometry of Physics, Volume 191.

I am currently working with Pramod Achar to give an explicit description of global Schubert varieties for GL(n). Preprint coming soon! In the near future, we would like to start expressing the theory of perverse sheaves in an infinity categorical framework.

My broad interests are in algebraic geometry, representation theory, and number theory. Recently, I am also interested in homotopy theory.
