Andrea Bourque

I used to study mathematical physics with Anton Zeitlin. See our paper "Flat GL(1|1)-connections and fatgraphs" here. This paper was published to the Journal of Geometry of Physics, Volume 191.

I am currently working with Pramod Achar to give an explicit description of global Schubert varieties for GL(n) in terms of lattices. Paper coming soon! Future work includes: using our descriptions to understand resolutions of singularities of global Schubert varieties, and extending our results to other groups, especially SL(n). There is also a family of open subsets of the special fiber of global Schubert varieties (at least in certain cases). I would like to show that they have nice properties, both intrinsic and in relation to the stratification by orbits.

My broad interests are in algebraic geometry, representation theory, and number theory. I also would like to explore homotopy theory and algebraic K-theory.

I have become particularly fond of perverse sheaves, thanks to Pramod. I wrote a lot about them on my blog.
